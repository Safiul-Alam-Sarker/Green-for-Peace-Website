What we do

Green for Peace,an organization of CUET for consciousness about nature conservation and pollution eradication. This organization is an environmental organization of Chittagong University of Engineering & Technology. We work to create consciousness for nature conservation and pollution eradication. It was established in 1998 by a group of innovative students. After establishment we are still working on various environment related issues. We arrange “Tree Plantation” program every year. We observe various significant days such as “Environment Day”, ”World Water Day”, “World Wildlife Day” etc. in our campus. To make people awake we arrange seminars on these days, serve leaflets, collect signature from teacher to student. We arrange periodically “Campus Cleaning Week”. Our volunteers encourage everyone to make neat and clean our campus. “Blood Donation” is also arranged by us as humanitarian work. We have made an online blood group database voluntarily where we are integrating blood group of all in our campus (www.gpblooddb.org). Whenever someone needs blood emergency we try to provide using our website. We also arrange winter relief campaign and collect relief and donation for the distressed people.
